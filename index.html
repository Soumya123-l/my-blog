Places must visit

Disney

I had visited it in May 2021 and November 2016

When I went to Disney in 2016, they had all the princesses and long lines. When I went in 2021 the longest wait time was 70 minustes because of COVID-19 restrictions. Overall I think Disney is a magical place and you should go here.



Universal

I had visited in May 2021

When I went to Universal, it was in the middle of a pandemic. I am so happy that we didn't have to wait three hours in line. I remember when we went to Universal that all the rides are scary and aren't babyish. Except a couple of rides. I recommend going to Univeral!



Wisconsin Dells

I had visited in 2016

In 2016 I was really little, nad that was a bummer because I was too short to ride the rides. When I went there I stilll had a lot fun. We had gone with another family to to Wisconsin Dells with us. I would reccomend going.
